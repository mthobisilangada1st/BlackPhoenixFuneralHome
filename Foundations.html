Siyamthanda Hope Foundation

We are proud partners of the Siyamthanda Hope Foundation, supporting families with children who are terminally ill due to cancer.

In loving memory of Siyamthanda Bonolo Khumalo (7), this foundation provides free funeral and advisory services to affected families.
