About Us

Black Phoenix Funeral Home was formed in 2015 with a clear and compassionate purpose: to serve families with empathy, care, and dignity during life’s most difficult moments.

Owner-managed and values-driven, we believe every life deserves a dignified send-off, regardless of circumstance. We offer affordable funeral policies underwritten by Ingwe Life and operate as a registered Financial Services Provider.

We are specialist repatriation service providers with partnerships across Southern Africa, the United Kingdom, Ireland, Canada, and the United States, ensuring loved ones are returned home with dignity.

Proud members of IFDA SA, we are committed to integrity, accountability, and professional excellence.
